## Definition
- For a [[Random Variable]] $\xi$ let
$$
  \phi_\xi(t) = \mathbb E e ^{t\xi}
$$

## Notes
- If $\xi$ has a [[1728426412-probability-density-function|pdf]] $p_\xi$ then by some theorem:
$$
  \phi_\xi(t) = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} e^{tx} p_\xi (x) dx
$$
