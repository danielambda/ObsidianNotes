## Definition
- Random Variable is a [[Function]] from [[Sample Space]] to Real Number $$
X:\Omega\to\mathbb R
$$
## Implications
$$\forall x \in \mathbb R: \set{\omega\in\Omega\,|\,X(\omega)\leq x}\in\mathfrak F$$
## Notation
- Uppercase letters
